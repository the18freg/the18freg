OUR STORY

Luxury Begins With Confidence

Every fragrance tells a story. THE18FREG was created to help people feel confident, lovable and unforgettable through premium alcohol-free perfume oils inspired by international luxury fragrances.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

You Deserve To Smell Good. That's Why We Exist.

THE18FREG was created with one simple belief — everyone deserves to experience premium fragrances without paying luxury prices.

Every fragrance is carefully crafted as an alcohol-free roll-on perfume oil, inspired by internationally loved fragrance profiles and designed to deliver long-lasting performance with a smooth skin-friendly application.

We believe fragrance is more than a scent. It is confidence, personality, memories and the first impression you leave behind.
